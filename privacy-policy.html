Privacy Policy

OnePreview is designed to preview files locally and avoid collecting personal information.

Information collection

OnePreview does not require an account and does not collect personal information, analytics, keystrokes, or file contents.

File handling

Files are opened through Apple system preview mechanisms and rendered locally on your device.

Network access

The preview renderer may request external resources referenced by a document, such as remote images or CDN-hosted rendering libraries used for Markdown, syntax highlighting, Mermaid, and KaTeX.

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or upload your documents to third parties.
