Privacy Policy

OnePreview is designed for local file preview and does not collect personal information.

Information collection

OnePreview does not require an account and does not collect analytics, personal information, keystrokes or file contents.

File handling

Files are opened through Apple system preview mechanisms and rendered locally on your device.

Network access

The preview renderer may request external resources referenced by a document, such as remote images or CDN-hosted rendering libraries used by Markdown features.

Third parties

We do not sell, share or upload your documents to third parties.
